A Tropical Twist on Scotch

Buchanan's Deluxe 12-Year Old offers a bright and smooth take on Scotch, with tropical fruit-forward notes that make it approachable and vibrant. From its refined character neat, to its refreshing evolution over ice, and its seamless integration into lively cocktails, Buchanan's celebrates the playful and versatile side of Scotch, perfect for perfect for a variety of cocktail types.
