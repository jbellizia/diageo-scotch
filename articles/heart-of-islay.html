Uncovering the Heart of Islay

Lagavulin 16-Year is an Islay icon, revered for its deep peat smoke, rugged coastal influences, and layers of earthy complexity. Whether enjoyed neat to capture its raw essence, softened over ice to reveal hidden sweetness, or as the foundation for bold, smoky cocktails, Lagavulin reflects the enduring spirit of Islay in every sip.

Neat

Showcasing Depth and Terroir

“Lagavulin's intense peat smoke and coastal influences create a layered experience. This Scotch embodies Islay's rugged spirit, balancing medicinal and earthy notes with sea salt hints,”– Colin Gordon, Master Distiller

Evolving the Experience

Cocktails

Lagavulin Highball

Lagavulin 16 year old over clear ice with chilled soda and a Meyer lemon or thyme accent. Elemental, smoky, and precise—this highball offers a restrained, aromatic take on Islay's power.

Lagavulin Spicy Sour

Lagavulin 16 year old's signature peat finds new life in this smoky, citrus-forward riff on a margarita. Balanced heat from jalapeño agave syrup adds layered depth without masking the whisky's bold character— a modern, flavorful twist that's both familiar and unmistakably Islay.
