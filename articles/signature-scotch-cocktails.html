Signature Scotch Cocktails

Tradition Meets Modern Mixology

Discover Scotch's versatility in the glass. This collection features full recipes from America's top whisky bars, developed to showcase simple, intentional serves that put Scotch front and center. Explore Johnnie Walker Black Label's smoky balance, Lagavulin 16 year old's powerful peaty character, Oban 14 year old's coastal elegance, and Buchanan's Deluxe 12 year old's smooth, tropical notes—each cocktail designed to celebrate its distinct character in an approachable, modern way.

Johnnie Walker Black Label

Black & Soda Highball

Johnnie Walker Black Label, soda, citrus twist—classic and crisp.

Recipe:

  • 1½ oz Johnnie Walker Black Label
  • Chilled soda water
  • Orange twist for garnish

Build in highball glass over long ice. Stir gently. Garnish with twist.

Black Old Fashioned

Smoky backbone, subtle spice, citrus lift.

Recipe:

  • 1½ oz Johnnie Walker Black Label
  • ¼ oz simple syrup
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • 1 dash orange bitters
  • Lemon or orange peel for garnish

Stir over ice. Strain onto large rock in Old Fashioned glass. Garnish with lemon or orange peel.

Black Rob Roy

Scotch's answer to the Manhattan.

Recipe:

  • 1½ oz Johnnie Walker Black Label
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Cherry for garnish

Stir over ice. Strain into chilled coupe. Garnish with cherry.

Lagavulin Highball

Lagavulin's smoky peat meets bright citrus.

Recipe:

  • 1½ oz Lagavulin 16-Year Old
  • 4 oz soda water
  • Lemon peel or thyme sprig for garnish

Build in highball glass over long ice. Stir lightly. Garnish with lemon peel or thyme.

Lagavulin Smoky Sour (Margarita)

Smoky peat, citrus, and subtle heat in an Islay-accented margarita riff.

Recipe:

  • 1¼ oz Lagavulin 16-Year
  • ½ oz lime
  • ½ oz jalapeño agave syrup
  • Lime for garnish

Add all ingredients into a cocktail shaker and shake for 8-10 seconds. Strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice with lime garnish.

Lagavulin Negroni

Bittersweet, peaty, sophisticated.

Recipe:

  • ½ oz Lagavulin 16-Year Old
  • ½ oz Campari
  • ½ oz sweet vermouth
  • Grapefruit twist for garnish

Stir over ice. Strain into coupe or over large rock. Garnish with grapefruit twist.

Oban 14 Year Old

Oban Highball

Smooth coastal character, citrus accent.

Recipe:

  • 1½ oz Oban 14-Year Old
  • 4 oz chilled soda water
  • Lemon peel or floral bitters for garnish

Build in highball glass over long ice. Stir gently. Garnish with lemon peel.

Oban Jungle Bird

Delightful tropical Scotch serve.

Recipe:

  • 1½ oz Oban 14-Year Old
  • ¾ oz pineapple juice
  • ¾ oz Campari
  • ½ oz fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ oz simple syrup
  • Pineapple frond and lime for garnish

Shake lightly with ice. Strain over large rock. Garnish with pineapple frond and/or lime.

Oban Paper Plane Riff

Bright and complex, modern classic twist.

Recipe:

  • 1 oz Oban 14-Year Old
  • 1 oz Amaro Nonino
  • 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1 oz Aperol or similar
  • Lemon peel for garnish

Shake with ice. Strain into coupe. Garnish with lemon peel.

Buchanan's 12 Year Old

Buchanita

Vibrant, crowd-friendly tropical note.

Recipe:

  • 1 oz Buchanan's Deluxe 12-Year Old
  • 2 oz pineapple juice
  • Pineapple wedge and/or leaf for garnish

Shake hard with ice for foam. Strain into rocks or coupe glass. Garnish with pineapple wedge and/or leaf.

Buchanan's Tropical Breeze

Bright, lush, and inviting.

Recipe:

  • 1½ oz Buchanan's 12-Year
  • 1 oz guava or mango nectar (or blend)
  • ½ oz grapefruit juice
  • Mint and/or grapefruit wedge

Shake Buchanan's, nectar, grapefruit juice, and syrup with ice. Strain over fresh ice in a rocks glass. Garnish with lime wheel or grapefruit wedge.

Buchanan's & Coconut

Unexpected, minimal, and delicious.

Recipe:

  • 1½ oz Buchanan's Deluxe 12-Year
  • Frozen coconut water ice cube(s)

Pour Scotch over coconut ice in rocks glass. No garnish needed.
