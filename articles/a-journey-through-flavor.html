A Journey Through Flavor

Johnnie Walker Black Label represents the art of blending at its finest. Made from malts sourced from the four corners of Scotland, the 12 year aged blend harmonizes deep layers of sweet fruit, spice, and vanilla - all wrapped in a cloak of smoke. From its bold character neat to its evolving flavors over ice, and its ability to inspire classic and contemporary serves. Black Label invites you to explore Scotch with both heritage and blending artistry in mind.

Neat

REVEALING CORE FLAVORS AND CRAFTSMANSHIP

“Drinking Johnnie Walker Black Label is like tasting sweetness wrapped in delicious smooth smoke. Experience the depth of sweet fruit and spice from distilleries like Clynelish in the Highlands and Cardhu on Speyside. Embrace creamy toffee and sweet vanilla from Lowland distilleries like Glenkinchie and wrap yourself in a cloak of smoke with Caol Ila from the remote Islands.” — Dr. Emma Walker, Johnnie Walker Master Blender

Rocks

A BRIEF PAUSE FOR SUBTLE SHIFTS

“Adding ice allows the Scotch's layers to evolve, softening bold flavors and letting hints of vanilla and spice rise. This shift reveals the craftsmanship that makes Black Label adaptable,”– Dr. Emma Walker, Johnnie Walker Master Blender

Cocktails

BLACK & SODA HIGHBALL

A clean, refreshing pour of Johnnie Walker Black Label over ice with chilled soda and citrus twist. This approachable highball lets smoky depth and subtle fruit notes shine with modern simplicity.

BLACK BOB ROY

Black Label stirred with sweet vermouth and bitters. A time-honored serve that respects tradition while showcasing balance, structure, and the elegant side of blended Scotch.
