Coastal Elegance in Every Sip

From its coastal home in western Scotland, Oban 14-Year Old is known for its harmonious balance of briny coastal notes, sea salt, and maritime citrus. Its versatility shines from neat pours that showcase its craftsmanship to highball creations that emphasize its herbal and coastal qualities Oban welcomes you to enjoy the delicious flavor that comes from 230 years of time-honoured tradition and heritage.
